Research shows that untreated children with anxiety disorders are at higher risk to perform poorly in school, miss out on important social experiences, and engage in substance abuse

Depression among children and adolescents is common but frequently unrecognized

Feelings are like clouds-they come and go and can have a lasting effect as much as the weather

Our Goal:

To create a safe space and means for children to express themselves through interactive inquries and activities to establish that all feelings are normal to experience


With the aid of schools our goal is to help elementary children

Features

Safe, comfortable, and confidential means to simplify the complexity of feelings and learning how to identify and express

Parental support to stay involved with overall patterns of emotions to help determine a proper recourse, once a pattern is determined

Teacher support with notifications to aid students at risk of repeated stressors, especially when teacher involvement may be the strongest benefit

How it works

Once a profile is created by the parent, your child can check in and engage with our chatbot on a daily schedule or as often as they would like. Our simplified icons help the child identify their feelings in a friendly manner. Our chatbot will provide suggested advice and videos based on phrases entered by the user.
