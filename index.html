About Feelings

Feelings can be complicated. They come and go as the clouds and can have a lasting effect as much as the weather

Did you know...

Depression among children and adolescents is common but frequently unrecognized affecting 2 percent of prepubertal children and 5 to 8 percent of adolescents.

Anxiety disorders affect one in eight children. Research shows that untreated children with anxiety disorders are at higher risk to perform poorly in school, miss out on important social experiences, and engage in substance abuse

Our Goal:

To create a safe space for children to express themselves through interactive inquries and activities to establish that all feelings are normal to experience
